About Me

Hello! I'm Vaishnavi Gannavaram, a dedicated Data Analyst with a Master’s degree in Health Data Science from Saint Louis University. My academic journey has provided me with a solid foundation in data analytics, focusing particularly on health data while also encompassing other domains. This has been enhanced by practical experience with advanced data-driven technologies.

During my master's program, I specialized in predictive modeling and machine learning, gaining proficiency in Python, R, and SQL. My internships at VICHAI, combined with my professional experience at Pharmalytics Group, allowed me to apply these skills in real-world settings. This included focusing on data cleaning, transformation, exploratory analysis, model development, visualization, and interpretation.

In addition to my professional experience, I have contributed to the academic community as a teaching assistant and active participant in analytics workshops. I am passionate about transforming complex data into actionable insights, with a strong emphasis on the healthcare industry but not limited to it, aiming to improve decision-making across various sectors. I am deeply committed to continuous learning and growth.
